Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Milk powder
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • 5 tablespoons milk
  • Oil for deep-frying
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon lemon juice, to prevent the sugar syrup from crystallizing.

Recipe

  1. Prepare the dough.
  2. Let the dough rest.
  3. Prepare the sugar syrup.
  4. Roll the jamuns.
  5. Fry the jamuns.
  6. Add jamuns to the sugar syrup.
  7. Garnish.

Kaju Barfi

Ingredients

  • 50 g Butter
  • 300 g Milk Powder
  • 100 g Sugar
  • ½ tsp Cardamom Powder
  • 200 ml Milk
  • Almonds crushed
  • Pistachios crushed

Recipe

  1. Add crushed almonds and pistachios.
  2. Add the butter and allow to melt completely.
  3. Add the milk and gradually allow it to heat until warm.
  4. Add the milk powder .Stir it continously
  5. Add the sugar and cardamom powder.
  6. Add mixture into the baking tin
  7. Serve alongside some freshly brewed tea and enjoy!

Ghevar

Ingredients

  • ½ Cup ghee
  • 1 block ice
  • 2 cup maida
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Silver vrak for garnishing

Recipe

  1. firstly, take ½ cup ghee and rub with ice block.
  2. Now add 2 cup maida .
  3. Pour the 2 tbsp of butter.
  4. The butter will splatter .
  5. Pour the sugar syrup over ghevar.
  6. Plate it carefully.
  7. Finally, garnish the ghevar with vrak and ready to serve.

Ghajar Halwa

Ingredients

  • 1 kg carrot
  • ¼ cup ghee
  • 10 cashew
  • 10 almonds
  • 3 cup milk
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup khoya
  • ¼ tsp cardamom powder

Recipe

  1. Firstly, peel the skin of carrot and grate finely.
  2. Fry until it turns golden brown. keep aside.
  3. Saute for 5 minutes or until it changes colour slightly.
  4. Now pour 3 cup milk and give a good stir.
  5. Cook until the halwa thickens .
  6. Mix well making sure everything is well combined.
  7. Finally, enjoy gajar ka halwa .

Boondi Laddu

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Gram Flour
  • 3 cups Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Rice Flour
  • ¼ tsp Baking Soda
  • ½ tsp Kesar
  • 1 tsp Cardamom Powder
  • 2 tsp Cashews
  • Cooking Oil for frying
  • 1½ cups Water

Recipe

  1. Heat a pan with 1/2 cup of water.
  2. Now add kesari powder and cardamom powder .
  3. Mix the gram flour, rice flour
  4. Fry the boondhi till they are 3/4th fried.
  5. Add it to the sugar syrup immediately.
  6. Add these roasted nuts to the boondhi syrup .
  7. Finally, The delicious laddu is ready to serve.
